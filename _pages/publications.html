Papers

Da Cunha, F. M. S., Araújo, M. M., de Melo, A. C. S., Lira, M. E. D. R., de Oliveira, A. S., Fonseca, F. S., ... & Dos Santos, W. P. (2025, November). Integrating ASTERI-Based EEG Imaging with Deep Learning and Swarm Intelligence for Autism Diagnosis Support. In 2025 IEEE Latin American Conference on Computational Intelligence (LA-CCI) (pp. 1–6). IEEE.
ABS | LINK

This work explores the integration of ASTERI-based EEG imaging with deep learning and swarm intelligence techniques to support autism diagnosis. The approach aims to improve classification performance by combining advanced signal processing with intelligent optimization strategies, contributing to more efficient and accessible diagnostic tools.
