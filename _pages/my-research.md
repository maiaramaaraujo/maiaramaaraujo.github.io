<div class="wordwrap">
<p>My research investigates how the brain represents, processes, and produces language, focusing on neural dynamics captured through EEG. Positioned at the intersection of neuroscience, language science, artificial intelligence, and human–machine interaction (HMI), my work explores how semantic, syntactic, and sensorimotor components of language are structured and emerge in neural activity. In particular, I focus on bridging representation and explainability by developing models whose internal representations are both informative and interpretable, enabling a more transparent and neurophysiologically grounded understanding of language processing.</p>
